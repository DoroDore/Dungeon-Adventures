You stand in front of a dungeon. As you try to peer into it's depths, you realize you can see nothing but darkness.
As you grip your weapon, you feel the steady thump of your heartbeat inside your chest. "I can do this." You tell your self. This brings you a bit of peace.
As you walk slowly into the mouth of the dungeon, you feel yourself engulfed in the darkness, as it seems to swallow you whole.
Then, you're falling. In the midst of the pitch darkness, you can't help but feel you've been here before...
